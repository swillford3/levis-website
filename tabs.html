What is the meaning behind the tab? The tab is a symbol of Levi's that the consumer is able to recognize at first site.

The Big 'E': Marks jeans that are pre-1971.

The Small 'E': Marks jeans that are post-1971. Changed from the big 'E' for easier identification.

The Silver Tab: Represents the grunge era and was around in the 80s and 90s.

The Orange Tab: Around during the 60s and 70s, is the most sought after tab color today.
